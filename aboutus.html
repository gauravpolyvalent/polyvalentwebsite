About Polyvalent Digital Services

The Beginning (November 2013)

We started our journey in November 2013 during a discussion between Geetika and Mansi. Recognizing the emerging gaps in digital marketing across their respective workplaces, they identified a significant opportunity to provide effective solutions. Geetika took the initial step, launching the company from a modest 200 sq ft room within her residence.

Early Growth (2015)

wo years marked a significant milestone as we moved to our new corporate office. This spacious 1000 sq ft office, boasting warm and inviting interiors, symbolized our initial growth. Interestingly, even with this expansion, Geetika and Mansi continued to foster a collaborative environment by choosing not to have separate cabins. Our team also grew to seven dedicated professionals, proudly serving 32 clients.

Expanding Horizons (2020)

By 2020, our team had significantly expanded to 30 talented individuals, serving over 200 clients. This growth necessitated a move to a larger, 3000 sq ft office. This period also marked our geographical expansion with a presence established in Mumbai. Our consistent growth reflects our dedication and commitment to providing excellent service.

Continued Evolution (2022)

This year was characterized by further progress. We moved into our own new, larger office space, welcoming many new faces to our growing team. This expansion fueled a variety of new projects and initiatives, with a strong focus on product innovation and development.

Present Day (2025)

Today, we are a team of over 50 professionals, collaborating with clients across diverse sectors. Rooted in our foundational principles, we continue to learn and evolve every day, deepening our expertise in technology, design, marketing, and, most importantly, understanding the needs of people.
