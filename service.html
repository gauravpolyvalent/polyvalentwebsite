Our Services

Online Brand Positioning

Polyvalent develops strong online brand positioning strategies for businesses to become unforgettable and easily discoverable. We define your voice, visualize your brand, identify your audience, and improve search rankings through market research, competitor analysis, and keyword mapping to help you dominate the search experience and establish a recognizable, searchable presence for sustainable growth.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) We Track:
  • Brand Awareness (e.g., direct traffic, social mentions, branded search volume)
  • Brand Sentiment (positive vs. negative mentions)
  • Website Traffic (organic and direct)
  • Audience Engagement Rates
  • Brand Recall and Recognition
  • Organic Search Rankings for target keywords
  • Visibility in SERPs (Search Engine Results Pages)
  • Click-through rate (CTR) from organic search results
